Welcome to the newly redesigned Multnomah Libertarians website. We'll be using this space to post meeting announcements, event recaps, and commentary on local and state issues from a libertarian perspective.

Our monthly meetups continue on the first Saturday of every month at Dick's Primal Burger, 4905 SE Woodstock Blvd, Portland, at 4:00 PM. All peaceful people are welcome — you do not need to be a registered Libertarian to attend.

For questions or to get in touch, email us at [member@multnomahlibertarians.com](mailto:member@multnomahlibertarians.com).
