What We Stand For

The Party of Principle

Libertarians oppose government interference in personal, family, and business decisions. Our principles can't be bought, negotiated, or intimidated.

01

Individual Liberty

Every person has the right to live their life as they see fit — as long as they impose no force or fraud on others.

02

Free Markets

Voluntary exchange and competition — not government control — create abundance and lasting prosperity.

03

Peace & Non-Intervention

A foreign policy of non-intervention, peace, and free trade. Cooperation without coercion, at home and abroad.

Multnomah Libertarians
